What is the Georgia Carolina Quiz Rally?

A quiz rally is a gathering of local churches from Georgia and the Carolinas in order for young people to compete in quizzing over a pre-determined and studied passage from the Bible.

A Quizmaster asks questions which can often only be answered using a very commanding memory of the exact phrasing of the passage, sometimes including word-perfect quotations.

Though the quizzing is the main focus, there is also time for food and fellowship, and the Word is preached at some point during the day.

There is a Spring and a Fall rally which are hosted by participating assemblies in rotation.
